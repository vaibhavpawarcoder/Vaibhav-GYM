HOW TO GET HERE

From Circular Quay, walk towards the Overseas Passenger Terminal, heading towards the Sydney Harbour Bridge, past the Museum of Contemporary Art.

Once you hit the Overseas Passenger, take the escalator or stairs up to the upper level.

Walk to the opposite end of the Overseas Passenger Terminal towards the Sydney Harbour Bridge.
